Features of our System

Hand Gesture Based

Because of hand gesture based, doctors provided with hygiene without touching the electronic gadgets for transforming the images

Time Saving System

Doctors may not be lead to manually work on transforming the images. Our system will be done this transformation through hand gesture, so it will save the time

Accurate System

It will predict the doctor's hand gestures accurately and transform the images respectively. Accurate transformation is the main advantage

High Performance

It will transform the images in a well optimized manner and transformation will take less time to perform
